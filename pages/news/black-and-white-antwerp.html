Black and White

Occasionally, when I encounter new people and collaborate with them for some time, they express surprise that I don’t always have a definitive stance on things. Initially, they anticipated me to be a person who thinks in black and white. But then I explain: I tend to think in a more colorful and nuanced way than most people.

But today, I decided to experiment with black-and-white photography. I revisited a series of colorful photos I took in Antwerp a few months ago and started playing with black and white. I explored every shade of gray in between to create a unique and captivating effect.
