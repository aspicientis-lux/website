Beavers

May 2024

De Biesbosch is the largest freshwater area in Europe that is influenced by tides, making it difficult for the rivers in the region to drain water to the sea during high tides. The area is home to many beavers, but spotting them requires a boat despite being I life close by. The beavers are most active around sunset, and I finally had the chance to see them yesterday.
