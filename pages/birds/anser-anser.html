Anser Anser

Noisy Troublemakers. That is an accurate characterization of the greylag goose. They often clash with Canada Geese (Branta Canadensis) or Egyptian Geese (Alopochen Aegyptiaca) when they share the same territory. They sometimes overrun a specific area and harm the environment.
