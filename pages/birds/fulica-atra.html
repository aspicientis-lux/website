Fulica Atra

Don’t let the size of this small water bird (Eurasian Coot) fool you, they are ferocious little creatures! They are fast, sometimes even aggressive, and afraid of nothing! In the past, I was working in an office with a pond in front of it. Always when the swans arrived all the other birds went away or hid themselves, but not this little guy, while the swans were 20 times bigger than them, they still tried to scare them away!
