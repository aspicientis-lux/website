Cyanistes Caeruleus

Watching Blue Tits is a lot of fun. They have a charming appearance, with a blue cap and a white face with a black stripe around the eyes. They look so adorable that you might wish to hug them. They are also very agile and can perform amazing feats of flight and climbing.

You'll often see Blue tits perched on branches, hanging upside down, or performing acrobatic maneuvers.

Seeds

They like to feed on insects when they are breeding, but they will also use their beaks to snatch seeds or other plant matter.

You will find them everywhere, in the forest, in parks and even in gardens, but there are fast and not as easy to catch in a photo as you may assume.
