Birds

Birds have always fascinated mankind. The Dream of Flight, is an inspiration for imagination, for technology, and even for music and choral composers. The freedom birds possess to go where ever they like, the songs some can sing, or even ‘laugh’ at you, and all the different species, makes that there is always something happing when you observe them.

Water

As I live in The Netherlands, water birds are everywhere due to the abundance of water. The reflective water during golden hour provides a perfect backdrop for capturing beautiful photographs.

Forrest

If you wish to listen to the natural symphony of the birds, visit a secluded woodland area in spring. You will be amazed by the variety of sounds, from the soft chirps of the tits to the melodious tunes of the robins. A peaceful stroll will turn into a musical adventure.

Technical

Birds are swift and erratic, and the lighting is often unpredictable. It is hard to capture clear, noise-free and beautiful images of them. It takes a lot of time and many discarded shots to get the perfect picture.

City

Urban areas are full of avian life, even though they can be a nuisance at times. If you look closely, you will discover many wonders.
